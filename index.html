What Is JPS Astronomy Club?

JPS Astronomy Club is a a group where students can build and apply their knowledge of astronomy through regular meetings, experiments, speaker events, research projects and competitions! Our goal is to work as a team to learn about the wonders of our world through fun activities, discussions and exciting events!

Areas of Focus

Research

Opportunities to collaborate with a team to take part in Mars Imaging projects using data from NASA to conduct research!

Astronomy-related events

Ranging from exciting observatory nights, launching weather balloons, stargazing nights, and science fairs, our events are filled with exploring beyond boundaries and having a great time!

Academic Competitions

Opportunities to exhibit your knowledge through astronomy competitions!

Newsletters

Opportunities to publish articles for our club's newsletter and partake as editors on the newsletter team!

Learning

A great way to build our knowledge through interactive speaker events, experiments and discussions!

Field Trips

A chance to attend exciting and fun field trips to learn more!
